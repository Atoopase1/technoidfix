1. Who We Are

Technoid is a tech services business based in Kumasi, Ghana, operated by Chris Atoopase. We provide device repair, electrical installation, graphic design, and web development services, and operate the websites at technoid.gh and related sub-domains.

When this policy refers to "Technoid," "we," "us," or "our," it means Chris Atoopase trading as Technoid.

2. Information We Collect

Information you give us:

  • Contact form submissions — your name, email address, phone number, and the content of your message when you submit a service enquiry or contact form.
  • Comments — if you leave a comment on our homepage, we store your email address and the comment text via our Supabase database.
  • Account sign-in — if you sign in via the Projects page to leave a comment, we store the email address associated with your account (via Supabase Auth).

Information collected automatically:

  • Standard web server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps) may be collected by our hosting provider.
  • We do not use Google Analytics or any third-party analytics tracking on this website.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect only for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your service enquiries and communicate about jobs or quotes.
  • To display approved comments on the Technoid homepage.
  • To verify your identity when you sign in to leave a comment or access admin features.
  • To improve the website and ensure it functions correctly.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We do not use your information for automated decision-making or profiling.

4. Data Storage & Third Parties

This website uses the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Supabase — we use Supabase (supabase.com) as our database and file storage provider. Comment data and user authentication are handled via Supabase. Their data is stored on servers in the EU or US depending on your region. See Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • Netlify / hosting — this site may be hosted on Netlify or a similar provider, which may log standard access data. See your provider's privacy policy for details.
  • Font Awesome & Google Fonts — icon and font resources are loaded from CDN providers, which may log your IP address for delivery purposes.
  • WhatsApp — if you click a WhatsApp button, you will be directed to WhatsApp's platform. Their own privacy policy applies once you leave our site.

All data held in our Supabase database is stored securely and only accessible to authorised Technoid admin accounts.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

This website uses minimal local storage for functional purposes only:

  • Theme preference — your dark/light mode choice is stored in your browser's localStorage so your preference is remembered across visits.
  • Session tokens — if you sign in via Supabase Auth, a session token is stored in your browser to keep you signed in. This expires when you sign out or after an inactivity period.

We do not use advertising cookies, marketing pixels, or cross-site tracking of any kind.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct any inaccurate information.
  • Delete your data — you can request that we remove your comments or contact data at any time.
  • Object to processing, where applicable.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the details below. We aim to respond within 7 business days.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. Access to the Supabase database is restricted to admin accounts only. Connections to this website use HTTPS encryption. However, no system is completely secure — if you have concerns about your data, contact us directly.

8. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information to us, please contact us immediately so we can remove it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in how we operate or changes in applicable law. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised accordingly. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
